Easy bake fish

Description

This is an easy fish recipe for people who spent a lot of money on salmon, but didn't buy the fancy sauce to go with it — a fishy delight!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 4 (6 ounce) salmon steaks
  • ½ teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix honey, mustard, and lemon juice. Spread mixture over salmon steaks. Season with pepper. Arrange in a medium baking dish.
  3. Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until fish easily flakes with a fork.
